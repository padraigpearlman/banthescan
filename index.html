The Problem

Surveillance wasn't built to be fair.

Facial recognition and biometric surveillance disproportionately targets BIPOC, Muslim, immigrant, and LGBTQ+ New Yorkers, and has already put innocent people in handcuffs.

01

Wrongful Arrests

Faulty matches have led to real people being wrongfully arrested and jailed, disproportionately Black New Yorkers.

02

Corporate Overreach

Madison Square Garden has used facial recognition to block attorneys at the door. Retailers like Wegmans and Rite Aid have tracked shoppers without consent.

03

It's Banned Elsewhere

Boston, San Francisco, Portland, and Minneapolis have already passed bans. New York should be next.

Statewide Biometric Surveillance Ban
